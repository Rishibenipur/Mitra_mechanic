We believe that buying a vehicle is a huge task, and we try to help our customers with every decision. We believe in maintaining long-term customers, customer satisfaction is of utmost importance for us. We have a dedicated team of professionals and have maintained resources to help our customers and make their task as easy as possible.

Showroom is located at NH 57 Darbhanga . We have full range of Mahindra cars displayed in the showroom. A wide range of genuine accessories is also well displayed for your selection. Our dedicated sales executives are always looking forward to welcome you and show the car of your choice, give a quote, offer best financing proposal, schedule a test drive or a service appointment. Our showroom has been designed in a way to make you feel comfortable and our sales representatives are always eager to answer your questions. Service Center is located at NH 57 Darbhanga. It is a custom built, state-of-the-art, fully equipped workshop with highly skilled mechanics. Genuine Parts is readily available for servicing the cars. Service and repairs are carried out strictly as per Mahindra norms by well qualified and Mahindra trained mechanics. Sales, Service and Support are the key ideals of our business.
